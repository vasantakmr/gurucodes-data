### About the Role:

Vercel is looking for a Community Engineer who will be responsible for interacting directly with our customers in the Vercel Community. More specifically, this role focuses heavily on working with customers on their code, helping by creating reproductions, sending pull requests, and working with our Engineering teams to resolve any issues that arise.

The role requires someone with both a background as a software engineer and just as importantly, a strong interest in a broad array of technologies to reflect the wide variety used by all Vercel customers. While Next.js experience is extremely valuable, you will need the ability to quickly pick up new frameworks based on the requirements of our customers.

Your impact within the Vercel Community will also stem beyond code and into platform use cases as well, driven by the the Vercel Community mission to help create the next generation of Vercel experts, you will learn many elements of the platform deeply, being able to use that knowledge to help customers find the ideal solution.

### What You Will Do:

Represent Vercel across our key communities, primarily in our official forums.

Build relationships with customers because you enjoy helping them to succeed.

Provide code-level assistance and work with customers to debug issues.

Use customer feedback to work with Product teams for platform improvements.

Create examples and patterns for how to achieve various goals using Vercel.

Learn a wide array of primarily frontend technologies, becoming a true expert.

Develop a strong level of expertise on both existing and new Vercel platform features.

Complete engineering tasks to improve the forum infrastructure and customer experience.
